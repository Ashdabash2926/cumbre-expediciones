Santa Cruz Trek

The classic Cordillera Blanca circuit through turquoise lakes, the Punta Union pass (4,750m), and lush valleys.

$250 per person

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Vaquería to Paria (3,850m) — gentle valley walk
  • Day 2: Paria to Taullipampa (4,250m) — Alpamayo views
  • Day 3: Punta Union Pass (4,750m) to Cashapampa
  • Day 4: Cashapampa to Huaraz (transfer)

What's Included

Certified guide, cook, porters, all meals, camping gear, transport to/from trailhead, emergency radio.

Huayhuash Circuit

One of the world's greatest treks. Remote wilderness, eight passes over 4,500m, and jaw-dropping scenery.

$650 per person

Itinerary Highlights

  • 10 days through Peru's most remote and dramatic mountain range
  • Eight passes above 4,500m including Cuyoc (5,000m)
  • Pristine glacial lakes: Carhuacocha, Jahuacocha, Gangrajanca
  • Views of Yerupajá (6,635m), Peru's second-highest peak

What's Included

Certified guide, cook, donkey team, all meals, camping gear, transport, emergency satellite phone, medical oxygen.
